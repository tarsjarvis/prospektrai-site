FAQ

Questions contractors ask
before they sign up.

Does Vicki actually sound like a real person?
Vicki communicates over SMS and chat — she doesn't call people back. Text-based responses are actually better for lead conversion because customers can reply when it's convenient. Her messages are written to feel natural and on-brand, not robotic.
What if a lead asks something Vicki can't answer?
She's trained on your specific trade and pricing. If something comes up that's outside her scope, she tells the lead that someone from your team will follow up directly — and alerts you immediately via Slack or SMS so you can step in.
How long does setup take?
Under a week. We handle everything — business line porting, Vicki training, calendar integration, and testing. You don't need any technical skills or software experience. Once it's live, it runs automatically.
What happens to my customer data?
Your lead data stays in our secured system — it is never sold, shared, or sent to third-party AI providers. We run our AI locally, not through OpenAI or any external cloud service.
Will this work for my trade?
We currently work with HVAC, roofing, plumbing, electrical, and general contractors. If your trade isn't listed, contact us — we've onboarded businesses across 18 service categories and can likely train Vicki for your specific workflow.
What if I already have a receptionist?
Vicki handles after-hours, overflow calls, and the leads your receptionist misses when they're busy. She works 24/7 — your receptionist doesn't. They complement each other, and most clients find they need fewer hours from human staff within 60 days.
Is there a contract or can I cancel anytime?
Month-to-month. No long-term contracts. You can cancel anytime with 30 days notice. We're confident the results speak for themselves — we don't need to lock you in.
How is this different from hiring a VA or answering service?
A VA costs $1,500–$3,000/month and works set hours. An answering service takes messages. Vicki responds in under 60 seconds, 24/7, qualifies leads, books appointments, AND follows up on cold estimates — for a fraction of the cost. She also never quits.
