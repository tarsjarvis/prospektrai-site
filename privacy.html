We collect publicly available business information including business name, phone number, email address, and industry category from public sources such as Google Maps and business directories. We do not collect sensitive personal information.

We use business contact information solely to reach out about our AI-powered call-handling and lead-response services. Each outreach message includes a clear opt-out mechanism.

3. SMS Communications

We may send SMS messages to business phone numbers identified through public listings. Every SMS message includes "Reply STOP to opt out." Recipients who reply STOP will be immediately removed from all future outreach and will receive no further messages. Message and data rates may apply.

4. Email Communications

We may send email to business addresses identified through public listings or submitted through our website. Each email includes an unsubscribe option. We honor all opt-out requests promptly.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes. We may use third-party service providers (such as Twilio for SMS and Google Sheets for data management) solely to operate our services.

6. Data Retention

We retain contact information only as long as necessary to provide our services or comply with legal obligations. Opt-out requests are honored immediately and permanently.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information we hold about you or your business by contacting us directly.
